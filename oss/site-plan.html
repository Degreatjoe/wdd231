Oka Secondary School Site Plan

Building a Legacy of Excellence

Site Name

Oka Secondary School Digital Portal

The name was selected to provide a sense of authority and modernity. It moves beyond a simple brochure site to suggest a functional "portal" for the local community, students, and alumni.

Site Purpose

The site serves as the official digital presence for Oka Secondary School. It provides historical context, a dynamic directory of school clubs and faculty to encourage student engagement, and a contact system to bridge the communication gap between the school administration and the alumni community.

Target Audience Scenarios

  • Prospective Parent: "What extracurricular clubs are available for my child, and who oversees them?"
  • Alumni Member: "How can I get in touch with the school to discuss a donation or a visit?"

Color Scheme

The palette is inspired by traditional academic excellence: Navy for authority and Gold for achievement.

Primary: #003366 (Navigation, Headers)

Secondary: #f2b705 (Accents, Buttons, Borders)

Background: #F4F4F4 (Main body background)

Typography

Heading Font: Roboto Slab

Used for titles and main headings to provide a formal, established feel.

Body Font: Open Sans

Used for all paragraph text and list items for high readability on all screen sizes.

Wireframes

Mobile View

Layout: Vertical stack. Hamburger menu, Hero image, Mission text, and a single-column news feed.

Desktop View

Layout: Horizontal navigation, wide hero banner, and a 3-column grid for Clubs and Faculty highlights.
