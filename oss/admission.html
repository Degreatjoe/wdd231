Admissions

Oka Secondary School welcomes students who are eager to grow academically and socially in a supportive learning environment.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Recent academic records
  • Birth certificate
  • Passport photographs

Enrollment Process

  1. Submit application form
  2. Attend entrance examination
  3. Interview with school administration
  4. Receive admission letter
